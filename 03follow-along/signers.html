Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include:

Thomas Jefferson
Main author of the Declaration of Independence and later the 3rd U.S. president.
John Adams
Strong advocate for independence and later the 2nd U.S. president.
Benjamin Franklin
Famous Founding Father known for his inventions, diplomacy, and writing.
John Hancock
Known for his large, bold signature at the top of the Declaration; his name became synonymous with signing documents.
Samuel Adams
Leader of the American Revolution and key figure in organizing resistance against British rule.
Roger Sherman
Helped draft the Declaration and contributed to shaping early American government.
Robert Morris
Financier of the American Revolution who helped fund the Continental Army.
Charles Carroll of Carrollton
The only Catholic signer of the Declaration of Independence.

Each of these individuals played a significant role in the founding of the United States and is remembered for their courage in signing the Declatation, an act considered treasonous by British Crown.
